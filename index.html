COVID-19 IN MALAYSIA

Last Updated: 05/10/2022

25 January 2020 marked the first day of COVID-19 pandemic in Malaysia. Since 5 May 2020, Ministry of Heath Malaysia (MOH) has been collecting data of COVID-19 in Malaysia. Here is some visualisation on the collected data.

Active COVID-19 Cases In Malaysia

Daily Active Cases in Malaysia for each state and each federal territories.

Deaths by COVID-19 In Malaysia

Total number of deaths by each state in the selected interval. There is a timeline to select the interval. The number of death are also represented using different colours. Black (>100), purple (1-100) and green (0).

COVID-19 Vaccinations In Malaysia

Daily vaccinations on 1st Dose and 2nd Dose have similar pattern as most of the population has been receiving their vaccinations in a 3 weeks interval. On 13 October 2021, Malaysia's government announced 1st Booster Dose administration. Hence, it start gaining popularity.
